Coach Dashboard Guide

Welcome to your Coach Dashboard! This is your central hub for monitoring your team's readiness and wellness. Use this guide to make the most of your dashboard.

Summary Metrics: At the top, you'll see key metrics for today including the Target Recovery Range (set via tags or team defaults), Team Average readiness score, Compliance percentage, and counts of athletes above, in, or below target range.

Squad Metrics Snapshot: This section shows average scores across all seven wellness categories (sleep, fatigue, soreness, stress, mood, energy, readiness). These help you identify patterns affecting the entire team.

Squad List: The main table shows each athlete's readiness score for today. Click any readiness circle to view their detailed seven-metric breakdown. Color coding indicates: Green = In target range, Orange = Below target, Purple = Above target.

Info Pills: Look for colored indicators next to player names: risk (below target 3+ days), rest (high soreness/low energy), non (missed reports), or MLT (multiple teams). Click any pill for details.

Date Navigation: Use the arrows to view different days. The Day Type shows the tag assigned to the selected date. You can also click player names to view their full dashboard and historical data.

Team Management: Use the navigation menu to access Team Schedule (set tags and target ranges), Tag Management (create custom day types), and other team settings.
