Athletes Self-Report Daily

Each day, your athletes log seven wellness metrics: sleep quality, energy levels, muscle soreness, mood/stress, motivation, nutrition, and hydration.

Condition Score Calculated

The app automatically calculates a Condition Score (0-100%) from these metrics using evidence-based weights. This score gives you a clear picture of each athlete's readiness to train or compete on any given day.

Track Your Team

Your dashboard shows the team's condition at a glance. See who's ready, who needs rest, and who might be at risk. Quickly identify athletes who are below target ranges or missing reports, so you can make informed decisions about training loads and match selection.

Plan & Schedule

Set up your team schedule with different day types (Training, Matches, Recovery, etc.) and target readiness ranges for each. The dashboard automatically highlights when athletes fall outside expected ranges, helping you optimize training and ensure peak performance when it matters most.
