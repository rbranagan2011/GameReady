1. Introduction

Welcome to GameReady ("we," "our," or "us"). GameReady is a wellness tracking platform designed to help athletes and coaches monitor daily wellness metrics to optimize training and reduce injury risk.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service. By using GameReady, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (used for login and communication)
  • First and last name
  • Password (stored securely using industry-standard hashing)
  • Role (Athlete or Coach)
  • Timezone (for daily reminder scheduling)

2.2 Wellness Data

As an athlete, you may submit daily readiness reports containing:

  • Sleep quality (1-10 scale)
  • Energy/fatigue levels (1-10 scale)
  • Muscle soreness/stiffness (1-10 scale)
  • Mood/stress levels (1-10 scale)
  • Motivation to train (1-10 scale)
  • Nutrition quality (1-10 scale)
  • Hydration status (1-10 scale)
  • Optional comments or notes
  • Player status (Available, Injured, Sick, Excused) with optional notes
  • Personal labels for specific days

This data is used to calculate a readiness score (0-100) that helps coaches make informed decisions about training.

2.3 Team Information

When you join or create a team, we collect:

  • Team name
  • Team join code
  • Team logo (if uploaded by coach)
  • Team schedule and tags (training days, game days, etc.)
  • Target readiness ranges

2.4 Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use GameReady:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Date and time of access

2.5 Cookies and Sessions

We use session cookies to keep you logged in. These cookies expire after 30 days of inactivity. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide and maintain the GameReady platform
  • Wellness Tracking: To calculate readiness scores and display wellness trends
  • Team Management: To allow coaches to view athlete data and manage team schedules
  • Communication: To send email verification, daily reminders, and important service updates
  • Account Management: To authenticate users and manage account settings
  • Analytics: To improve our service and understand usage patterns (using PostHog, if enabled)
  • Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Within Your Team

Coaches can see: All wellness data, readiness scores, player status, and personal labels submitted by athletes on their team(s).

Athletes can see: Their own wellness data and readiness scores. Athletes cannot see other athletes' data unless they are also a coach.

4.2 Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform:

  • Email Service (SendGrid): To send verification emails and daily reminders
  • Hosting Provider (Render): To host our application and database
  • Analytics (PostHog, optional): To analyze usage patterns and improve our service

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests, such as:

  • Court orders or subpoenas
  • Government investigations
  • Protection of our rights or the safety of our users

4.4 No Sale of Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or wellness data to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
  • Data is transmitted over HTTPS (SSL/TLS encryption)
  • Database access is restricted and monitored
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Secure session management

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services:

  • Account Data: Retained until account deletion
  • Wellness Reports: Retained until account deletion or team removal
  • Team Data: Retained until team is deleted by coach or administrator
  • Logs: Retained for up to 90 days for security and debugging purposes

When you delete your account, all associated wellness data, reports, and personal information are permanently deleted from our systems.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can view all your wellness data and account information through your dashboard
  • Correction: You can update your profile information and email address in Account Settings
  • Deletion: You can request account deletion, which will permanently remove all your data
  • Data Export: You can request a copy of your data (contact support for assistance)
  • Opt-Out: You can disable daily reminder emails in your account settings
  • Team Removal: You can leave a team at any time, which removes your association with that team

To exercise these rights, please use the Account Settings page or contact us at admin@gamereadyapp.com.

8. Children's Privacy

GameReady is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country. By using GameReady, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
